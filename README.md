# SMAQ
## Smart monitoring of air quality
* Temperature
* Pressure
* Humidity
* Light
* Carbon monoxide CO 1 – 1000ppm
* Nitrogen dioxide NO2 0.05 - 10ppm
* Ethanol C2H5OH 10 – 500ppm
* Hydrogen H2 1 – 1000ppm
* Ammonia NH3 1 – 500ppm
* Methane CH4 >1000ppm
* Propane C3H8 >1000ppm
* Iso-butane C4H10 >1000ppm
